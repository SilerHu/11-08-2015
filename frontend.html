Descriptions: We applied two approaches, Random Forest (randomForest, http://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/randomForest/index.html) and MACH (v1), to test the imputation accuracy for missing values in our association mapping panel. First we constructed a genotyping dataset contained 2758 SNPs selected from the 92K iSelect genotyping assay, including 1207 mapped to 3A and 1551 mapped to 3B. From this dataset, different level of missing values (NA20, NA30, NA40, NA50, NA60) were then simulated by random picking of genotyped values to be set as un-genotyped. A total of 10 datasets for each level of missing were generated. The imputed genotypes were then compare to the original genotypes to infer the accuracy of imputation.
